Helen Moseley is a highly committed soccer coach and leader in grassroots football, with nearly a decade of experience coaching in the Northern New South Wales region. She has been a coach with North Coast Football since 2016, focusing on developing young players’ skills, confidence and love for the game. In addition to her coaching, Helen works in women’s and girls’ participation roles, helping create inclusive and supportive environments that encourage ongoing engagement in soccer. Her contributions extend beyond the pitch, where she actively champions growth and opportunities for players and coaches alike.
